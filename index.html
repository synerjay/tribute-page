Terry Fox

A Canadian hero who ran across the country for cancer research.

This is his remarkeable story.

Terry Fox embarking on a cross-country journey called the "Marathon of Hope" to raise awareness and charity to cancer research.

His Life and Legacy

  • Terry Fox was born on July 28, 1958, in Winnipeg, Manitoba, to Rolland and Betty Fox.
  • March 1977 - Fox was diagnosed with osteosarcoma, a form of cancer that often starts near the knees.
  • April 12, 1980 - Fox embarked on a country-wide marathon called the "Marathon of Hope" to raise funds and awareness to cancer research
  • September 1, 1980 outside Thunder Bay, Fox was forced to stop briefly after he suffered an intense coughing fit and experienced pains in his chest.
  • June 28, 1981 - a month before his 23rd birthday with his family by his side, Fox died from complications of pneumonia.
